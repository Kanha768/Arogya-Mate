Who We Are

Arogya Mate is dedicated to providing accessible healthcare information and emergency services inspired by Ayushman Bharat. Our mission is to connect you with trusted health guidance, vital emergency numbers, and government health benefits all in one platform.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families with reliable health information, quick access to emergency services, and awareness about government health schemes. We aim to be your trusted partner in health and wellness.

Our Vision

To create a healthier society where everyone has easy access to quality healthcare guidance, emergency support, and government benefits, ensuring no one is left behind.
